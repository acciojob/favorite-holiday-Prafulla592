HYDERABAD


Hyderabad, the most populous and biggest city in the Telangana state, is the largest urban area in entire south-central India. Between 1956 and 2014, when Telangana, a state that is an extension of Andhra Pradesh, was created, Hyderabad served as the state of Andhra Pradesh's capital.
